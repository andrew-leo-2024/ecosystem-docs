The AINEFF Ecosystem documentation site is now live — **124 pages** of constitutional infrastructure documentation spanning from 40,000ft vision down to microscopic technical architecture.

## What's Inside

This documentation represents the complete specification for the AINEFF Ecosystem — a constitutional economic coordination protocol designed to become civilization-scale infrastructure.

### The Altitude Model

The docs are organized across zoom levels:

- **40,000ft** — Vision, philosophy, and the Atomic Constraint
- **20,000ft** — Ecosystem blueprint, entity hierarchy, and coordination layers
- **10,000ft** — Entity definitions (AINEFF, AINEG, AINEF-OS, FrankMax, AINEs)
- **5,000ft** — 21 core systems and module specifications
- **1,000ft** — Products, revenue streams, pricing architecture, and market wedges
- **Ground** — Execution plans, KPI dashboards, risk registers, and financial models
- **Micro** — Technical architecture, agent design patterns, protocol specs
- **Substrate** — Knowledge foundation, thinking tools, belief systems

### 363 Mermaid Diagrams

Every system, process, and relationship is visualized with Mermaid diagrams — making the ecosystem's structure immediately graspable at any zoom level.

### 20+ Standard Operating Procedures

From governance reviews to incident response, every operational process is documented with step-by-step procedures, BPMN flows, and escalation paths.
